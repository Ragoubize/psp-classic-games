Frequently Asked Questions

How can I play PSP games on my Android phone?
You can play PSP games on Android using a free emulator app like PPSSPP. Download it from the Play Store, transfer PSP game files (.iso or .cso) to your device, then open them in the emulator.
Do I need a powerful phone to run PSP games?
Most modern Android phones handle PSP games smoothly, but for the best results, use a device with at least 2GB RAM and a mid-range or higher processor.
Can I use a controller when playing on mobile?
Yes! Most popular Bluetooth controllers are supported by emulators such as PPSSPP. Just pair your controller, change the emulator's input settings, and enjoy classic gameplay.
Where do I find PSP game files?
We share information about classic titles, but do not distribute copyrighted files. Please acquire games from legal sources you own.
